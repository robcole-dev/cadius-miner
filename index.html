Cadius Miner

How to play

Introduction

Cadius Miner is a asteroid based shooting game. The objective is to mine as many asteroids as possible. Each asteroid successfully mined scores you 5 points. However, becareful as these asteroids are dangerous. A single hit to your ships hull will cause extreme damage!

Controls

    For PC Users

  • Move Left - Use the Left arrow key
  • Move Right - Use the Right arrow Key
  • To shoot - Use the space bar

    For Mobile or Tablet Users

  • Move left or Right - Touch the ship and drag left or right
  • To shoot - Touch anywhere on the screen with a second finger
