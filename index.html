How to play Play

Introduction


Cadius Miner is an asteroid based shooting game. The objective is to mine as many asteroids as possible. Each asteroid successfully mined scores you 5 points. However, be careful as these asteroids are dangerous. A single hit to your shipâ€™s hull will cause extreme damage!


Controls


For PC Users


  • Move Left - Use the Left arrow key
  • Move Right - Use the Right arrow Key
  • To shoot - Use the space bar

For Mobile or Tablet Users


  • Move left or Right - Touch the ship and drag it left or right
  • To shoot - Touch anywhere on the screen with a second finger
